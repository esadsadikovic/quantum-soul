# The Quantum Soul: Consciousness and Subconsciousness Entangled in Ether

[![DOI](https://zenodo.org/badge/DOI/10.5281/zenodo.19911973.svg)](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19911973)

## 📄 Abstract
This paper explores the concept of the "quantum soul," an interdisciplinary framework bridging philosophy of consciousness, speculative physics, and Jungian psychology. It examines how quantum principles may provide metaphors or mechanisms for understanding subjective experience, archetypes, and the continuity of identity.

The work is intended as a starting point for dialogue between researchers in philosophy, psychology, and physics. By presenting original synthesis rather than definitive claims, it invites others to critique, refine, and expand the framework.
